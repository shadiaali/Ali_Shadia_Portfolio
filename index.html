About Me

Boozhoo! Nice to meet you! My name is Shadia Ursula. I am a full stack Interactive Media Specialist with a solid educational and real-world foundation in web development, digital marketing, branding, design and ux/ui. I am passionate about bringing ideas to life — from conceptualization to creation.

I consider telling stories to be the most important part of what I do. Storytelling communicates fundamental human experiences, which unite and connect us all.

As a multicultural, Indigenous Canadian, my passion for storytelling runs deeply. It is an excellent method for learning, as well as a powerful, strategic tool for transmitting knowledge and meaning.

Every project has a story to tell the user.

I graduated from Fanshawe’s award-winning Interactive Media & Design program in 2019, and obtained my graduate certification in Interactive Media Specialist in 2020.

When I am not coding, you can find me:

  • A  Volunteering with ngtc

    ngtc

    I'm the Founder, full stack developer & account/project coordinator of NGTC - a collective of new grads serving their community by volunteerism through tech.

    NGTC is a pro-bono, volunteer based freelance collective. Members of NGTC donate their time and skills to non-profits, businesses and individuals in order to give back to the community, as well as gain experience in this difficult job market. Members of NGTC have graduated from programs in the Interactive Media/Graphic design cluster, and are trained and adept at digital media and multimedia production; this includes web development (app development, project management and digital communications, concept and planning, back-end and front-end), branding and identity, layout and design, and content creation.

    Our website, ngtc.tech is currently in development and coming soon!
